# Discovery Task — Interview Guide & Template

## Interview Questions

### Hypothesis & Context
- What's the hypothesis or question you want to answer?
- What prompted this discovery? (customer signal, metric change, strategic question)
- What do we already know or believe?

### Research Questions
- What specific questions need answering?
- What would change your mind about the direction?
- What's the riskiest assumption?

### Methodology
- What type of research makes sense? (user interviews, data analysis, competitive research, prototype testing, survey)
- Who should we talk to or what data should we look at?
- Do we need any tools or access?

### Expected Outputs & Timeline
- What deliverable comes out of this? (report, recommendation, PRD, decision)
- Who needs to review the findings?
- What's the timeline?
