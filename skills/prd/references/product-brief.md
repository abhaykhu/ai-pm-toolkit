# Product Brief — Interview Guide & Template

## Interview Questions

### Opportunity
- What's the opportunity? (new market, unmet need, competitive gap)
- How big is the opportunity? (users affected, revenue potential, strategic value)
- What evidence supports this?

### Proposed Solution
- What's the high-level proposed solution?
- How is this different from what exists today?
- What's the core value proposition?

### Market Context
- Who else is doing this? How?
- What's our unique advantage?
- Are there market trends making this timely?

### Success & Resources
- What does success look like in 6-12 months?
- What resources are needed? (team, budget, timeline)
- What are the key risks?
